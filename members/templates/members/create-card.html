Create A Membership Card

Create a new card if:
  • You don't already have one.
  • You had one but you lost it.
  • You think your card might have been copied.

Creating a new card will invalidate your previous card, so don't download a new card if you already have an uncompromised card that you like.

After printing the membership card and/or saving it to your thumb drive, you must locate the PDF file on this computer and delete it. If you don't, other users of this computer will be able to use it to impersonate you. If you are unsure how to locate and delete the file, please ask for help!
