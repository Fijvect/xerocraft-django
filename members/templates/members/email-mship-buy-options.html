Paid Memberships

The simplest option is to pay full dues on a monthly basis, with discounts for 3 or 6 month purchases:

1 month for $50
3 month for $132
6 month for $225

Work-Trade Memberships

If you have time to donate to the upkeep and operation of Xerocraft, you may be interested in one of the following Work-Trade options:

1 month for $25 + 6 volunteer hrs
1 month for $10 + 9 volunteer hrs
2 hours for $0 + 1 volunteer hr

Volunteer work must be coordinated in advance with Xerocraft's staff. Contact volunteer@xerocraft.org if you have questions about Work-Trade or if you'd like to enroll in the program.
